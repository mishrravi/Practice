India vs Australia Highlights 4th Test, Day 2: IND reach 36/0 at Stumps after Ashwin takes 6 to bowl out AUS for 480

IND vs AUS Highlights, 4th Test, Day 2: In response to Australia's first innings total of 480, India reached 36/0 at Stumps. Catch highlights of India vs Australia 4th Test Day 2 here.

IND vs AUS Highlights, 4th Test, Day 2: With Ravichandran Ashwin taking six wickets, Australia were bowled out for 480 in the first innings vs India on Day 2 of the ongoing fourth and final Test match, at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad on Friday. In response to Australia's first innings score, India reached 36/0 in 10 overs at Stumps. Rohit Sharma (17*) and Shubman Gill (18*) will resume batting for India on Day 3. Day 2 began with Australia building on their overnight score of 255/4. Cameron Green reached his maiden Test ton with ease. Green was removed by Ashwin, who also took the wickets of Alex Carey and mitchell Starc in the post-Lunch session. Meanwhile, Axar Patel struck after Tea to remove Usman Khawaja, who slammed 180 off 422 balls. Ashwin completed his five-wicket haul with the wicket of Todd Murphy and then removed Nathan Lyon for his sixth wicket.
